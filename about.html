About Me

Hello!

My name is ZoieAnn Baird and I am a multimedia artist and designer. I am a Digital Media: Visual Imaging Major from Northwest Missouri State University. I am also the founder of Missing Socks Studios, an animation and webcomic studio specializing in fantasy and adventure. I was born and raised in Saint Joseph, Missouri, and started my art journey at 10 years old. I have always had a creative spark from a young age. I was very involved in dance, music, and theatre growing up, and was always had a designers eye.

I love exploring different styles and media. Some of my favorites include animation, oil painting, and character design. I have a great ability to be able to replicate multiple styles of artwork closely. My artwork ranges from multiple different styles such as realism, to more stylized pieces of work. I aim to expand this even more in the future!

Thank you for coming to support my page!
